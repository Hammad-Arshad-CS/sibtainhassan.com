About Dr. Syed Sibtain ul Hassan

Dr. Syed Sibtain ul Hassan is a board-certified Consultant Physician and Internal Medicine Specialist based in Lahore, Pakistan, with over a decade of experience in managing complex medical conditions. He is renowned for his expert care in diabetes, metabolic disorders, liver diseases, thyroid dysfunction, and hypertension.


Dr. Hassan holds an MBBS degree, FCPS in Medicine from the College of Physicians & Surgeons Pakistan (CPSP), and is USA-Certified in Diabetology — reflecting his commitment to global standards in chronic disease management


He practices at Omar Hospital & Cardiac Centre and Ihsan Mumtaz Hospital in Johar Town, Lahore, and also offers online telemedicine consultations for national and international patients.

Clinical Philosophy

Dr. Hassan emphasizes a personalized, evidence-based approach. Each treatment plan integrates the latest medical standards with lifestyle and nutrition guidance to deliver meaningful health improvements.
